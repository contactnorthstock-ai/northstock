What is a dividend?

A dividend is a cash payment companies make to shareholders, usually every 3 months. If you own 100 shares of Royal Bank (RY.TO) at a 3.26% yield and the stock is $174, you'd receive approximately $567 per year — without selling a single share. In a TFSA, this income is completely tax-free.
