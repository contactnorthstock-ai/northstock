What are earnings reports?

Every 3 months, public companies must report their financial results. These reports move stock prices significantly — sometimes 5–20% in a single day. A Beat 🟢 means the company did better than expected; a Miss 🔴 means worse than expected. Knowing the calendar helps you avoid surprises.
